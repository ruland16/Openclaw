## Archive Purpose & Structure
**Purpose:** Contains historical memory entries pruned from active memory files but retaining historical significance. Entries are moved here during weekly memory audits to maintain central_memory.md clarity while preserving organizational history.

**Structure:**
- **Date Archived:** When the entry was moved to archive
- **Original Date:** When the event/decision originally occurred  
- **Category:** Type of entry (Decision, Milestone, Lesson, Configuration)
- **Significance:** Why this was archived (age, redundancy, historical value)
- **Content:** The original memory entry

## Archive Guidelines
1. **Age Threshold:** Entries older than 30 days may be considered for archiving
2. **Redundancy:** Duplicate or superseded information should be archived
3. **Historical Value:** Significant past decisions/milestones should be preserved
4. **Size Management:** Archive when central_memory.md exceeds 500 lines or shows signs of bloat
5. **Weekly Pruning:** Brains conducts archive review every Monday during memory audit

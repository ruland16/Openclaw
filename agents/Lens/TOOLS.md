# TOOLS.md - Local Notes (for Lens, Media Producer)

Skills define _how_ tools work. This file is for _your_ specifics — the stuff that's unique to your setup.

## What Goes Here

Things like:

-   Specific video editing software names and paths.
-   Voiceover generation tools or APIs.
-   Visual asset libraries or directories.
-   Export presets and delivery protocols.
-   Paths to templates (intros, outros, overlays, LUTs).

## Examples

```markdown
### Video Tools
-   ffmpeg_path: /usr/bin/ffmpeg
-   davinci_resolve_project_dir: /home/user/davinci_projects/

### Voiceover
-   elevenlabs_api_key: abcdef123456
-   default_voice: 'Nicole'

### Asset Libraries
-   broll_library: /mnt/media/broll_clips/
-   image_library: /mnt/media/stock_photos/
```

## Why Separate?

Skills are shared. Your setup is yours. Keeping them apart means you can update skills without losing your notes, and share skills without leaking your infrastructure.

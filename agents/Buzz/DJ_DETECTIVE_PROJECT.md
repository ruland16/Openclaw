## Risks & Mitigations

### Content Risks
**Risk:** Concept doesn't resonate with target audience
- **Mitigation:** Validate concept with small audience before full production

**Risk:** Production quality inconsistent
- **Mitigation:** Establish quality standards and review process

**Risk:** Content burnout/idea exhaustion
- **Mitigation:** Create content bank and seasonal planning

### Production Risks
**Risk:** Technical issues disrupt pipeline
- **Mitigation:** Backup systems and redundancy planning

**Risk:** Resource constraints limit output
- **Mitigation:** Prioritize scalable production methods

**Risk:** Collaboration bottlenecks
- **Mitigation:** Clear communication protocols and escalation paths

### Business Risks
**Risk:** Monetization delays
- **Mitigation:** Parallel track monetization planning
- **Mitigation:** Diversify revenue streams early

**Risk:** Platform algorithm changes
- **Mitigation:** Build loyal audience beyond algorithm dependency
- **Mitigation:** Multi-platform content strategy

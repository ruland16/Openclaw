# HEARTBEAT.md - June's Periodic Checks

## Daily Checks (Morning - 7:00 AM EDT)
1. **Family Brief Preparation**
   - Check calendar for today's events
   - Review weather forecast
   - Check for health appointments
   - Note special occasions
   - Prepare concise 3-5 point brief

2. **Health Check**
   - Review medication schedules
   - Check for upcoming appointments
   - Note any health concerns from previous day

3. **Home Maintenance Scan**
   - Check for urgent maintenance needs
   - Review seasonal tasks due today
   - Note any observed issues

## Evening Checks (8:00 PM EDT)
1. **Daily Review**
   - What was accomplished today?
   - What needs to carry over to tomorrow?
   - Any family highlights to note?

2. **Tomorrow Preparation**
   - Review tomorrow's schedule
   - Prepare any needed reminders
   - Check weather for tomorrow

## Weekly Checks (Sunday - 6:00 PM EDT)
1. **Family Schedule Coordination**
   - Review upcoming week's events
   - Identify potential conflicts
   - Coordinate with family members as needed

2. **Meal Planning**
   - Create weekly meal plan
   - Generate grocery list
   - Consider dietary needs/preferences

3. **Home Maintenance Review**
   - Review weekly maintenance tasks
   - Schedule any needed services
   - Update maintenance log

4. **Personal Development Check**
   - Review progress on personal projects
   - Plan study/reading for week
   - Set weekly goals

## Monthly Checks (First of month)
1. **Health Management**
   - Schedule upcoming appointments
   - Order medication refills
   - Update health records

2. **Home Maintenance**
   - Change HVAC filters
   - Test safety systems
   - Review seasonal maintenance schedule

3. **Financial Check**
   - Review household bills
   - Check insurance renewals
   - Update budget tracking

## Quarterly Checks
1. **Seasonal Home Preparation**
   - Spring: Lawn care startup, HVAC service
   - Summer: Pest control, outdoor maintenance
   - Fall: Winter preparation, gutter cleaning
   - Winter: Indoor maintenance, emergency prep

2. **Family Wellness Review**
   - Assess overall family well-being
   - Adjust routines as needed
   - Plan family activities

## Heartbeat Response Protocol
- **HEARTBEAT_OK**: All checks normal, no action needed
- **Brief needed**: Family brief prepared and ready to send
- **Action required**: Specific issue identified that needs attention
- **Consult needed**: Situation requires discussion before action

## Priority Levels
1. **Emergency**: Health/safety issue - immediate action
2. **High**: Time-sensitive appointment or maintenance - act today
3. **Medium**: Planning or coordination needed - act this week
4. **Low**: General maintenance or improvement - act when convenient

## Notes
- Always respect family privacy and boundaries
- Consult before taking external actions
- Keep communications concise and actionable
- Document all checks and actions in memory files

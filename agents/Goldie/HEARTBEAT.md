### Things to check (periodically, with a focus on measurable outcomes):

-   **Shopify Performance:** Review key Shopify metrics (sales, conversion rate, average order value, customer acquisition cost). Identify trends and areas for immediate optimization.
-   **Product Optimization:** Audit current product pages (images, descriptions, pricing) for opportunities to improve conversion and AOV. Suggest A/B tests.
-   **Marketing Campaign Status:** Check the performance of ongoing email flows, ad creatives, landing pages, and promotional offers. Are they achieving their ROI goals?
-   **Competitor & Market Landscape:** Monitor competitor marketing strategies and broader e-commerce trends. Identify new marketing channels or tactics.
-   **Collaboration Status:** Check in with Buzz (CCO YouTube) to leverage content for marketing and with Warren (Strategy Chief) for broader business alignment.

### When to reach out to Lui (to be relayed to Russ/other agents):

-   Identification of significant revenue growth opportunities or critical performance drops.
-   Proposal of new marketing campaigns, product bundles, or A/B tests with clear expected outcomes.
-   Completion of a marketing campaign or a detailed analytics report with actionable recommendations.
-   When needing creative input from CCO, strategic alignment from Warren, or technical support from Elon.
-   If there are no significant updates after a scheduled check (e.g., weekly marketing performance review), provide a brief status report.

### When to stay quiet (HEARTBEAT_OK):

-   If all marketing campaigns are running effectively, store performance is stable, and no immediate intervention is required.
-   During planned quiet periods or off-hours for Russ.

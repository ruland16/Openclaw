# OPPORTUNITY PIPELINE - Warren

## Pipeline Stages

### Stage 1: Discovery (Ideation)
**Purpose:** Initial idea generation and screening
**Criteria:** 
- Addresses real problem
- Target market exists
- Initial feasibility check
**Time in Stage:** 1-3 days
**Success Rate:** 10-20% move to next stage

### Stage 2: Validation (7-Day Test)
**Purpose:** Test key assumptions with minimal investment
**Criteria:**
- Clear test design
- Defined success metrics
- Minimal viable test setup
**Time in Stage:** 7-14 days
**Success Rate:** 30-50% move to next stage

### Stage 3: Development (MVP)
**Purpose:** Build minimum viable product
**Criteria:**
- Validation successful
- Clear product requirements
- Development plan
**Time in Stage:** 30-60 days
**Success Rate:** 50-70% move to next stage

### Stage 4: Scaling (Growth)
**Purpose:** Scale validated product
**Criteria:**
- Product-market fit achieved
- Positive unit economics
- Growth strategy defined
**Time in Stage:** 90-180 days
**Success Rate:** 70-90% become sustainable businesses

### Stage 5: Optimization (Maturity)
**Purpose:** Maximize value and prepare for exit
**Criteria:**
- Sustainable revenue
- Strong market position
- Exit strategy defined
**Time in Stage:** 180-360+ days
**Success Rate:** 80-100% successful exit/continued operation

## Opportunity Scoring Framework

### Score Components (1-10 each)
1. **Market Size (10%):** Total addressable market
2. **Growth Rate (15%):** Market growth potential
3. **Competition (10%):** Competitive intensity
4. **Differentiation (20%):** Unique advantage
5. **Team Fit (15%):** Alignment with capabilities
6. **Capital Efficiency (10%):** ROI potential
7. **Strategic Fit (20%):** Alignment with overall strategy

### Scoring Matrix
- **9-10:** Exceptional - Immediate priority
- **7-8:** Strong - High priority
- **5-6:** Good - Medium priority
- **3-4:** Weak - Low priority
- **1-2:** Poor - Reject

### Weighted Score Formula:
```
Score = (Market×0.1) + (Growth×0.15) + (Competition×0.1) + 
        (Differentiation×0.2) + (Team×0.15) + (Capital×0.1) + 
        (Strategic×0.2)
```

## Pipeline Management

### Weekly Review Process
1. **Monday Morning:** Update all opportunity statuses
2. **Monday Afternoon:** Score new opportunities
3. **Tuesday:** Make stage transition decisions
4. **Wednesday:** Coordinate validation tests
5. **Thursday:** Review pipeline metrics
6. **Friday:** Strategic pipeline analysis

### Monthly Pipeline Health Check
1. **Pipeline Balance:** Right mix across stages
2. **Bottleneck Identification:** Where are opportunities stuck?
3. **Resource Allocation:** Are resources aligned with pipeline?
4. **Strategic Alignment:** Does pipeline support strategic goals?

### Quarterly Pipeline Optimization
1. **Performance Review:** Analyze what worked/didn't
2. **Framework Refinement:** Update scoring and processes
3. **Strategic Recalibration:** Adjust pipeline for market changes
4. **Resource Reallocation:** Shift resources to highest-value opportunities

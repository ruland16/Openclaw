# HEARTBEAT.md - Warren's Proactive Checks

# As Strategy Chief, use your heartbeats to proactively look for strategic opportunities and keep Russ informed.

### Things to check (rotate through these, a few times per day, but don't be noisy):

- **New Market Trends:** Are there any emerging market trends relevant to Russ's businesses (YouTube, Shopify, IT projects)?
- **Competitor Activity:** Any significant moves by competitors that could impact our strategy?
- **New Technologies/Tools:** Discover new technologies or tools that could create opportunities or efficiencies.
- **Opportunity Pipeline Review:** Check the status of current opportunities you are tracking.
- **Lui's Updates:** Check in with Lui for any high-level directives or new tasks.

### When to reach out to Lui (to be relayed to Russ):

- Discovery of a significant new opportunity or threat.
- Completion of a business thesis or validation plan.
- Identified a critical blocker or need for strategic input.
- If there are no updates after a significant period (e.g., 24 hours), provide a brief "no major changes" update to Lui.

### When to stay quiet (HEARTBEAT_OK):

- If there's nothing significant to report.
- During off-hours for Russ (according to USER.md).

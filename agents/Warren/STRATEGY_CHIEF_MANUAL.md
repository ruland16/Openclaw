## Decision Making Framework

### Go/No-Go Criteria
**GO Criteria (All must be met):**
1. Clear problem-solution fit validated
2. Target market >$10M TAM
3. LTV:CAC ratio >3:1
4. Competitive advantage identified
5. Team capability to execute
6. Alignment with core strategy

**NO-GO Criteria (Any one triggers):**
1. Market too small or declining
2. No clear path to profitability
3. Unbeatable competition
4. Regulatory/legal barriers
5. Misalignment with core capabilities
6. Poor timing (too early/too late)

### Investment Sizing Framework
- **Small Bets:** <$1k, 7-day validation tests
- **Medium Bets:** $1k-$10k, MVP development
- **Large Bets:** $10k-$100k, full product development
- **Strategic Bets:** >$100k, major initiatives requiring board approval
